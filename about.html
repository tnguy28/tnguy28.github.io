About Me

I have created multiple websites and apps that meet consumer needs of gaming content guides by providing short, simplified information to reach wide audiences. In addition, I have five years of experience in social media content creation and regularly entertain an audience of 1000+ subscribers through a unique approach in games.

I find these activities very relaxing due to their pacing, especially since IT can be highly stressful at certain points. I love continuing the hobbies mentioned above, but I am always open to exploring new opportunities as well.
