General Ethics for IT Professionals

As more people are starting to integrate technology into their lives, information has become a valuable source because it helps people make informed decisions on any given situation. While information can be used to benefit society, bad actors can also exploit it to further their own agendas whether personal or political. Ethics is essential in guiding acceptable behaviors of a company and by following these practices we can continue to have a safe digital environment.

1.Privacy of Information

Users should be aware of what information is strictly being collected and nothing else.

2.Benefits to Society

Any work or contributions should be made to benefit the general public and avoid harming any other groups.

3.Transparency

Informing users of potential updates or incidents is important to maintain integrity to the general public.

4.Security

Information should be kept safe and regular checks should be made to keep up with persistent security threats.
